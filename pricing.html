What Affects Your Final Price

Multiple factors determine the exact cost of your cleaning service. Here's what we consider when providing your quote:

Property Size

Square footage and room count

  • Number of bedrooms and bathrooms
  • Total floor area
  • Number of separate living spaces

Current Condition

Level of soiling and maintenance

  • Light maintenance vs. heavy buildup
  • Time since last professional cleaning
  • Visible grime, stains, or neglected areas

Service Frequency

One-time vs. recurring service

  • One-time services may cost more
  • Weekly/bi-weekly clients may receive preferred rates
  • Long-term contracts may qualify for discounts

Location

Distance from our base in El Salvador City

  • Properties outside El Salvador City may incur travel fees
  • Remote or difficult-to-access locations may cost more
  • Some barangays in extended service area subject to availability

Add-On Services

Additional tasks beyond base tier

  • Each add-on service has separate pricing
  • Some add-ons may increase total service time significantly
  • Must be requested at booking, not day-of

Property Accessibility

Access challenges or special requirements

  • Multi-story homes without elevator
  • Limited parking or long walk from vehicle
  • Gated communities requiring special entry procedures

Special Supplies

Client-requested cleaning products

  • We provide standard supplies at no extra charge
  • Hypoallergenic or specialty products (if we supply) may cost more
  • Client may provide their own products at no charge

Time Constraints

Scheduling limitations

  • Same-week or rush bookings may have premium pricing
  • Weekend or evening services may cost more
  • Standard weekday bookings receive standard rates

Peak Season Pricing

During high-demand periods, pricing may be adjusted to reflect increased scheduling constraints and service demand. Peak seasons typically include:

  • Holiday Season: Late November through early January
  • Holy Week: Week before Easter
  • Summer Move Season: April through May (school year transitions)
  • End-of-Year: Mid-December through New Year

During these periods, base prices may increase by 15-30%. We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance during peak seasons to secure preferred rates and availability.

Pricing Transparency

We Will Quote Your Exact Price

After you submit a booking request, we'll review your property details and provide a specific price quote before you commit. The quote will include:

  • Base service tier price
  • Any applicable add-on charges
  • Travel or accessibility fees (if any)
  • Peak season adjustments (if applicable)
  • Estimated service time

No Hidden Fees

The price we quote is the price you pay. We do not add surprise charges after service unless:

  • You request additional work beyond the agreed scope on the day of service
  • We encounter undisclosed conditions (e.g., severe biohazard, safety issues) that require additional time or supplies
  • Access issues prevent completion as scheduled

In such cases, we will inform you of additional charges before proceeding.

Deposit Requirement

All bookings require a 50% non-refundable deposit to reserve your service slot. The remaining 50% is due upon service completion. See our Payment Policy for full details.

No Guarantees

While we strive to provide accurate estimates, actual cleaning time and results may vary based on factors discovered on-site. We do not guarantee specific outcomes, time frames, or pricing beyond what is stated in your confirmed quote.
