Service Policies

Clear expectations and procedures for all services

These policies apply to all services provided by Northline Cleaning Services. By booking our services, you agree to these terms. Please read carefully before scheduling.

Booking & Payment Policy

Deposit Requirement

All cleaning services require a 50% deposit at the time of booking confirmation. This deposit:

  • Reserves your specific date and time slot in our schedule
  • Covers our scheduling and administrative costs
  • Commits our team's availability to your service
  • Is non-refundable under all circumstances

The deposit is not refundable even if you cancel for any reason.

Final Payment

The remaining 50% balance is due immediately upon completion of service. Accepted payment methods:

  • Cash (exact amount preferred)
  • Bank transfer (GCash, Maya, or direct bank deposit)
  • Check (with valid ID, subject to clearance)

Service is not considered complete until final payment is received in full.

Booking Confirmation

Your booking is confirmed only after:

  1. You submit a complete booking request
  2. We provide a price quote
  3. You accept the quote
  4. You pay the 50% deposit
  5. We send written confirmation via email or Messenger

Verbal agreements or incomplete requests do not constitute confirmed bookings.

Price Quote Validity

Price quotes are valid for 7 days from issuance. If you do not book within this period, we may need to reassess pricing based on current scheduling and availability.

Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

Cancellation by Client

All cancellations result in forfeiture of the 50% deposit. This applies regardless of:

  • How far in advance you cancel
  • The reason for cancellation (emergency, change of plans, etc.)
  • Whether you intend to reschedule

The deposit compensates us for the reserved time slot and lost opportunity to serve other clients.

One-Time Rescheduling

You may reschedule your service one time only without losing your deposit, subject to these conditions:

  • You must provide at least 48 hours advance notice
  • Rescheduling must be to a date within 30 days of original booking
  • New date is subject to our availability
  • You must accept the rescheduled date we offer

Important: If you reschedule and then cancel or fail to reschedule again, your deposit is forfeited. There is no second rescheduling opportunity.

Same-Day Cancellation

If you cancel on the day of service (for any reason, including emergencies), your deposit is forfeited and no rescheduling is permitted. This includes:

  • Canceling the morning of your scheduled service
  • Not being present when our team arrives
  • Denying access to your property on the service date
  • Requesting cancellation after our team has departed to your location

No-Show Policy

If you are not present or do not provide access at your scheduled service time, this is considered a no-show. Your deposit is forfeited and you will be charged a ₱500 trip fee in addition to the lost deposit. You will not be permitted to book future services until this fee is paid.

Cancellation by Northline Cleaning Services

We reserve the right to cancel service for:

  • Unsafe working conditions
  • Undisclosed property conditions (hazards, extreme hoarding, etc.)
  • Client behavior that is threatening or disrespectful
  • Weather emergencies or force majeure events

If we cancel for these reasons after arriving on-site, your deposit is forfeited. If we cancel before the service date due to our scheduling issues or force majeure, your deposit will be fully refunded or applied to a rescheduled service at your choice.

Quality Assurance & Re-Clean Policy

Our Quality Commitment

We aim to complete all cleaning tasks according to your chosen service tier. However, we do not guarantee perfection or specific outcomes. Cleaning results can vary based on existing stains, material conditions, and other factors.

Reporting Issues

If you are unsatisfied with the cleaning, you must notify us within 24 hours of service completion. Contact us via:

  • Email: vaughn.saculinggan@gmail.com
  • Facebook Messenger: Northline Cleaning Services

Include specific details and, if possible, photos of the areas of concern.

Free Re-Clean (One Time Only)

If we determine that tasks within your service tier were missed or not completed to standard, we will provide one free re-clean subject to these conditions:

  • Issue is reported within 24 hours
  • The problem area was included in your original service tier
  • The issue is due to our oversight, not property conditions
  • Re-clean must be scheduled within 7 days of original service
  • You must be present or provide access for re-clean

The re-clean is limited strictly to the specific areas or tasks that were missed. It is not a full repeat of the entire service.

What Is NOT Covered by Re-Clean

  • Tasks not included in your service tier
  • Areas you did not disclose at booking (e.g., a room that was locked)
  • Stains or buildup that cannot be fully removed
  • Personal preference differences (e.g., "not clean enough" without specific missed tasks)
  • Damage or wear that cleaning cannot fix
  • Issues reported after 24 hours

No Refunds After Service Completion

Once service is completed and final payment is made, we do not provide refunds. Your sole remedy for quality issues is the one-time free re-clean as described above.

Client Responsibilities During Service

To ensure quality results:

  • Remove clutter and personal items from surfaces before we arrive
  • Secure pets or keep them away from work areas
  • Disclose all areas to be cleaned at booking time
  • Provide access to water, electricity, and cleaning areas
  • Empty refrigerator/cabinets if interior cleaning is requested

Failure to meet these responsibilities may affect cleaning outcomes and is not grounds for re-clean or refund.

Cleaning Products & Allergy Notice

Standard Cleaning Products

We use professional-grade, commercially available cleaning products for all services. Our standard supplies include:

  • Multi-surface cleaners
  • Disinfectants and sanitizers
  • Glass cleaners
  • Bathroom cleaners (tile, toilet, tub)
  • Floor cleaners
  • Degreasers for kitchen surfaces

These products are effective for most cleaning tasks and are provided at no additional charge.

Allergy & Sensitivity Disclosure

You must disclose any allergies, sensitivities, or product restrictions at the time of booking. This includes:

  • Allergies to specific chemicals or fragrances
  • Respiratory sensitivities (asthma, etc.)
  • Skin sensitivities
  • Pet sensitivities to certain products
  • Preference for unscented or natural products

If you do not disclose these issues at booking and experience a reaction, we cannot be held liable.

Special Product Requests

If you require specific cleaning products due to allergies or preferences, you have two options:

Option 1: Client Provides Products

You may supply your own cleaning products at no additional charge. You must provide all necessary products in sufficient quantities for the service. We are not responsible if your products are ineffective or unsuitable for the tasks.

Option 2: We Source Special Products

If you request that we provide hypoallergenic, eco-friendly, or specific brand products, an additional fee will apply. This fee covers:

  • Cost of specialty products
  • Time to source and purchase products
  • Any delivery or procurement costs

The additional fee will be quoted before booking confirmation. This must be arranged in advance; we cannot accommodate same-day special product requests.

Product Safety & Effectiveness

If you provide your own products or request we use specific products:

  • We cannot guarantee cleaning effectiveness if products are unsuitable for the tasks
  • We may refuse to use products we determine to be unsafe or ineffective
  • Cleaning results may vary compared to our standard professional products
  • This does not entitle you to a refund, discount, or re-clean

Liability for Reactions

Northline Cleaning Services is not liable for allergic reactions or sensitivities if:

  • You did not disclose the allergy or sensitivity at booking
  • You approved the use of standard products
  • You provided the products that caused the reaction
  • The reaction occurred after the service was completed

If you have known sensitivities, it is your responsibility to disclose them and make appropriate arrangements.

Service Limitations & Exclusions

What We Do Not Provide

For safety, legal, and capability reasons, we do not provide the following services under any circumstances:

❌ Medical or Caregiving Services

  • No personal care for individuals (bathing, feeding, dressing)
  • No medication administration
  • No health monitoring or medical tasks
  • No pet care or pet grooming

❌ Hazardous Material Cleanup

  • No blood or bodily fluid cleanup
  • No biohazard or medical waste removal
  • No chemical spills or toxic material handling
  • No sewage or contaminated water cleanup

❌ Mold & Pest Issues

  • No mold remediation or treatment
  • No removal of extensive mold growth
  • No pest control or extermination
  • No cleanup of active infestations

Light surface mildew can be cleaned. Extensive mold requires licensed remediation specialists.

❌ Repairs & Maintenance

  • No plumbing repairs
  • No electrical work
  • No appliance repair or maintenance
  • No furniture assembly or repair
  • No painting or wall repair

❌ High-Risk Exterior Work

  • No gutter cleaning
  • No roof cleaning or maintenance
  • No exterior window washing above first floor
  • No pressure washing
  • No work requiring ladders above 6 feet

❌ Hoarding & Extreme Conditions

  • No hoarding cleanup (requires specialized services)
  • No spaces with blocked exits or unsafe pathways
  • No properties with structural hazards
  • No spaces with inadequate ventilation

❌ Specialized Tasks

  • No carpet shampooing or steam cleaning
  • No upholstery deep cleaning
  • No tile regrouting or sealing
  • No wood floor refinishing or waxing
  • No HVAC duct cleaning

On-Site Refusal of Service

If we arrive and encounter conditions that fall under these limitations or pose safety risks, we reserve the right to:

  • Refuse to perform the service
  • Modify the scope to exclude hazardous or impossible tasks
  • Leave the premises without completing service

In these cases, your deposit is forfeited. We cannot refund deposits when service cannot be completed due to undisclosed or hazardous conditions.

High-Reach Cleaning

We can clean high-reach areas up to 8 feet using extension tools. This includes:

  • Ceiling fans (up to 8 feet)
  • Top of tall furniture (if safely reachable)
  • High windows accessible from floor with extension tool

We cannot clean areas requiring:

  • Ladders taller than 6 feet
  • Scaffolding or elevated platforms
  • Anything above 8 feet
  • Access that poses a fall risk

Liability & Damage Policy

Client Property

We take reasonable care to avoid damage to your property. However, accidents may occur. Our liability is limited as follows:

  • We are liable for damage directly caused by negligence or misuse of equipment
  • We are NOT liable for pre-existing damage discovered during cleaning
  • We are NOT liable for damage to fragile items left in cleaning areas
  • Maximum liability per incident is ₱5,000 or the cost of repair, whichever is less

You must report any damage within 24 hours with photographic evidence. Claims made after 24 hours will not be accepted.

Securing Valuables

You are responsible for securing valuables, cash, jewelry, documents, and fragile items before service. We are not liable for:

  • Loss or theft of unsecured valuables
  • Damage to items left in cleaning areas
  • Breakage of fragile decorative items

Access & Security

If you provide us keys, access codes, or security information:

  • We will safeguard this information and use it only for scheduled services
  • You are responsible for changing codes or locks after service ends
  • We are not liable for security breaches that occur after our access period
