Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us via email or Facebook Messenger.

You can book in two ways:

  1. Online Form: Fill out our booking form with your details, property information, and preferred date. We'll review and send you a price quote within 24 hours.
  2. Facebook Messenger: Message us directly with your booking request and we'll guide you through the process.

Once you receive and accept the quote, you'll pay a 50% deposit to confirm your booking.

The 50% deposit serves several purposes:

  • It reserves your specific date and time in our schedule
  • It commits our team's availability exclusively to your service
  • It prevents lost revenue when clients cancel last-minute
  • It covers our administrative and scheduling costs

The deposit is non-refundable under all circumstances to protect our business from scheduling losses.

  • How far in advance you cancel
  • The reason for cancellation (emergency, illness, change of plans)
  • Whether you intend to reschedule later

You may reschedule one time with at least 48 hours notice without losing your deposit. See our Cancellation Policy for details.

Pricing depends on several factors:

  • Service tier (Basic, Standard, or Deep Cleaning)
  • Property size and room count
  • Current condition of the space
  • Your location (distance from El Salvador City)
  • Any add-on services requested
  • Peak season timing

Price ranges:

  • Basic Cleaning: ₱800 - ₱1,500
  • Standard Cleaning: ₱1,500 - ₱3,000
  • Deep Cleaning: ₱2,500 - ₱5,000+

These are estimates only. We provide an exact quote after you submit a booking request. See our Pricing Page for more details.

Basic Cleaning: Essential maintenance for already clean spaces. Includes sweeping, mopping, dusting, bathroom sanitizing, and trash removal. Does not include deep scrubbing or detailed work.

Standard Cleaning: Comprehensive cleaning for most needs. Everything in Basic plus deep kitchen cleaning, window wiping, appliance exteriors, baseboard cleaning, and bathroom tile scrubbing.

Deep Cleaning: Intensive cleaning for neglected or heavily soiled spaces. Everything in Standard plus inside refrigerator/oven, all baseboards, ceiling fans, behind appliances, detailed grout cleaning, and light fixtures.

Review the Services Page for complete lists of inclusions and exclusions for each tier.

If tasks within your service tier were missed or not completed to standard:

  1. Contact us within 24 hours of service completion
  2. Provide specific details and photos if possible
  3. We'll evaluate and, if warranted, schedule a free re-clean

Important: The re-clean covers only the specific missed tasks, not a full repeat service. It must be scheduled within 7 days of the original service.

We do not provide refunds after service completion. The one-time re-clean is your sole remedy for quality issues.

Service time varies based on property size, condition, and service tier:
