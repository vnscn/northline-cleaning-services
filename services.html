Our Services

Choose the cleaning level that fits your needs

We offer three distinct service tiers for residential and small office cleaning. Each tier has specific inclusions and exclusions to ensure clear expectations. Review each option carefully and select the one that matches your cleaning needs.

Important: Services are performed as specified in each tier. Additional requests beyond the stated scope may incur extra charges or may not be available. Prices depend on 1 cleaner, 2 cleaners, or 3 cleaners; larger homes require more cleaners or longer time. Scope is limited by booked manpower—not all tasks may be completed if scope exceeds time; additional time = additional charge.

Basic Cleaning

Essential maintenance cleaning for regularly maintained spaces

✓ Included

  • Sweep and mop all accessible floors
  • Dust all reachable surfaces (tables, counters, shelves)
  • Wipe down kitchen counters and sinks
  • Clean and sanitize bathroom fixtures (toilet, sink, shower/tub exterior)
  • Empty and replace trash bags in all rooms
  • Spot clean visible marks on walls (within reach)
  • Light tidying of items left out

✗ Not Included

  • Deep scrubbing of appliances
  • Inside cabinets, drawers, or closets
  • Window washing (exterior or interior)
  • Moving furniture
  • Organizing or decluttering
  • Baseboard or ceiling fan cleaning
  • Washing dishes or laundry
  • High-reach areas (ceiling corners, top of tall furniture)

Best for: Weekly or bi-weekly maintenance of already clean spaces

Standard Cleaning

Comprehensive cleaning for most residential and office needs

✓ Included

  • Everything in Basic Cleaning, plus:
  • Deep clean kitchen (stovetop, oven exterior, microwave interior, refrigerator exterior)
  • Scrub bathroom tiles and grout (shower/tub walls)
  • Clean interior windows (accessible from floor level)
  • Wipe down appliance exteriors
  • Dust blinds and curtains (reachable areas)
  • Clean baseboards (main living areas)
  • Vacuum or sweep under light furniture (chairs, small tables)

✗ Not Included

  • Inside refrigerator or oven
  • Inside cabinets or drawers
  • Exterior window washing
  • Heavy furniture moving
  • Organizing or storage arrangement
  • Ceiling fan blades (high reach)
  • Behind/under large appliances
  • High dusting (above 6 feet)

Best for: Monthly deep cleaning or move-in/move-out situations

Deep Cleaning

Intensive, detailed cleaning for neglected or heavily soiled spaces

✓ Included

  • Everything in Standard Cleaning, plus:
  • Inside refrigerator (client must empty contents)
  • Inside oven (standard residential ovens only)
  • Inside cabinets (exterior wipe-down only; client must empty)
  • Behind accessible appliances (if can be moved safely)
  • All baseboards throughout the space
  • Ceiling fan blades (using extension tools, up to 8 feet)
  • Light fixture exteriors (reachable or removable)
  • Door frames and light switches
  • Detailed grout cleaning (bathroom and kitchen)

✗ Not Included

  • Organizing or arranging items inside cabinets
  • Washing curtains or removing blinds for cleaning
  • Carpet shampooing or steam cleaning
  • Exterior window washing
  • Mold remediation
  • Hoarding or excessive clutter situations
  • High-reach areas above 8 feet
  • Pest control or infestation cleanup

Best for: Quarterly intensive cleaning, post-renovation, or long-neglected spaces

Add-On Services

Add-ons are not included in base packages and must be requested in advance. Additional cost applies.

  • Laundry (wash/dry/fold)
  • Refrigerator interior cleaning
  • Oven/stove deep clean
  • Cabinet / closet organizing
  • Mattress sanitizing
  • Window cleaning (reachable areas only)

Add-On Services

These services can be added to any tier. All add-ons require advance notice at booking and incur additional charges.

Laundry Service

Washing, drying, and folding of clothes. Client must provide detergent and have functioning washer/dryer on-site.

Does not include ironing or specialized garment care.

Refrigerator Deep Clean

Complete interior cleaning of refrigerator and freezer. Client must empty all contents before service begins.

Available only with Standard or Deep Cleaning packages.

Interior Window Washing (Extended)

All interior windows including second-story or hard-to-reach locations (if safely accessible).

Exterior windows not included. Safety assessment required.

Blinds Deep Clean

Removal, cleaning, and reinstallation of blinds (standard horizontal or vertical blinds only).

Does not include curtain washing or specialty window treatments.

Post-Event Cleanup

Cleaning after parties, gatherings, or events. Includes trash removal, surface cleaning, and floor care.

Does not include outdoor areas or extreme mess requiring specialized disposal.

Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning

Intensive cleaning of vacant properties. Space must be completely empty of furniture and belongings.

Requires Deep Cleaning tier. May require extra time depending on property condition.

Important Add-On Information

  • All add-ons must be requested at the time of booking
  • Add-ons cannot be added on the day of service
  • Pricing for add-ons varies based on scope and property size
  • Some add-ons may extend total service time
  • We reserve the right to refuse add-on services if conditions are unsafe or beyond our capability

Service Limitations & Time Boundaries

Services are limited by time booked and number of cleaners. Not all tasks may be completed if scope exceeds time. Additional time = additional charge.

For safety, legal, and capability reasons, we do not provide the following services:

❌ Medical or Personal Care

No bathing, feeding, medication administration, or any caregiving tasks for people or pets.

❌ Hazardous Material

No cleaning of blood, bodily fluids, chemical spills, or any biohazardous or toxic materials.

❌ Mold Remediation

No mold removal or treatment. Surface mildew cleaning only. Extensive mold requires licensed remediation.

❌ Pest Control

No removal or cleanup of infestations (insects, rodents). Active infestations require pest control services first.

❌ Repairs or Maintenance

No plumbing, electrical, appliance repair, or any construction/handyman work.

❌ High-Reach Exterior Work

No gutter cleaning, roof cleaning, exterior window washing above first floor, or other high-risk exterior work.

❌ Hoarding Situations

Spaces with excessive clutter, blocked pathways, or unsafe conditions require specialized services.

❌ Unvented or Hazardous Spaces

No cleaning of spaces with poor ventilation, asbestos, lead paint, or other environmental hazards.

Note: If we arrive on-site and encounter conditions that fall under these limitations, we reserve the right to refuse service or modify the scope. Any deposit paid will be subject to our cancellation policy. For unusual situations, contact us before booking to discuss feasibility.
